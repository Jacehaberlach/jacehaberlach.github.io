The Best Recipe to make Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin Pie is my most favorite out of all of the pies my Grandfather makes almost everytime they visit us or we visit them he wants to make a pie and he asks what kind and I always say Pumpkin pie its just the way that he makes them. - My Dad

Pumpkin Pie ingredients

  • Store-bought uncooked pie crust
  • Canned pumpkin
  • Canned sweetened condensed milk
  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin pie spice
  • Salt

Steps to make the Pie

  1. Set oven rack in lowest position of oven and heat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Thaw the chilled pie crust according to package directions, unroll and then fit into pie dish. Decorate as desired (I fold edges under just barely and push edges between fingertips to create a wavy design).
  3. In a mixing bowl whisk together pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, pumpkin pie spice and salt until well blended.
  4. Once the oven is at its set temp place the pie into the oven for 1 hour
  5. Once cooked transfer to a wire rack for at least 2 hours
