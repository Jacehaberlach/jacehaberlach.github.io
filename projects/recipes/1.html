The Best Recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

Chocolate Chip Cookies and Cookie dough me and my sister both love them so much that whenever my sister comes home from Virginia Tech she wants to bake and mix some ingredients with me and most of the time we have extra cookie dough that we don't put into the oven and we just eat off of the mixing bowl. Thats why i'm going to be making a recipe on how to make Chocolate chip cookie dough.

The ingredients that you will need for each of the steps are in the list below these are for the original recipe for 36 servings

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

I have compiled the three steps for the recipe in the list below

  1. Mix butter, milk, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl.
  2. Combine flour, brown sugar, white sugar, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl; slowly mix into butter mixture, beating on low speed with an electric mixer until dough is just combined.
  3. Fold chocolate chips into dough; drop by rounded tablespoon onto waxed paper.
  4. Recipe Tip: You can add more flour if the dough is too soft or sticky; add more milk if the dough is too stiff.

There is a potential risk of foodborne illness from the consumption of raw flour
