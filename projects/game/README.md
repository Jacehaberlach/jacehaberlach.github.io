I am going to make the Hypixel Sky-block minecraft hub with many different places to go and side-quests in the hub to upgrade your profile to be the best.


Sky craft Scrutiny


You start in the Sky craft hub where you have to talk to the NPC's to figure out where you are why and how to make money and grow your profile to become the best in the entire game.

Directory Map:
![Map of the game](DirectoryMap.svg)
On the map there will be 5 islands in sky craft each that you have to visit to collect enough of the requirements to have the museum entry not kill you after you make it to the forest of lost souls.

Global variable descriptions:

	You will also have an inventory that has a number to represent what you have. The weapons you have to get are worth 5 points. The zombie head is worth 20 points.

	To have the museum not kill you, and to open the secret passage you have to have your inventory  variable be set to 25 points.

	The gotWeapons variable is used to check if you already have weapons when you go into the blacksmith shop so then you can't just get infinite weapons after leaving and coming back multiple times.
